Sliders & rails¶

Pure-Python port of BOSL2’s sliders.scad: a V-groove slider() and the matching rail(), both designed to 3-D print without support. Tune the printed fit with the slider’s slop.

V-groove sliders and rails (BOSL2 sliders.scad).

static slider(l=30, w=10, h=10, base=10, wall=5, ang=30, slop=0.0)[source]¶

A slider that rides in a matching rail() V-groove (BOSL2 slider()).

Examples

A slider:

from bosl2.sliders import Sliders
Sliders.slider(l=30, base=10, wall=4, slop=0.2).show()
